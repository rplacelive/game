Privacy policy


We do not sell or offer your personal information to any 3rd parties.

We do however, for the site's functionality, collect the following information:

IP addresses & Session identifiers

This is used to enforce cooldown and protect against bots, and is not stored beyond the length of your session

Usernames and account names you tell us

This should be self-explanatory. We store this data until you tell us not to by closing your account or changing said details.

Chat messages and pixel placements

By posting chat messages or drawing art, you accept that this type of content is permanent and cannot be removed, even if requested.

Science Telemetrics

We occasionally collect non-identifying information about your hardware or behavior in the game for research purposes, such as developing new features or technologies to improve the experience of everyone in the game
